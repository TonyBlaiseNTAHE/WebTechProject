About Us

The Adventist University of Central Africa (AUCA) known as MUDENDE is a Seventh-day Adventist Institution of Higher Learning which has a philosophy to operate on
the basis of the Seventh-day Adventist worldview:”God is the Creator and Sustainer of the universe and the source of true knowledge.
The entrance of sin caused man’s alienation from God, therefore the restoration of the relation between man and his God
is the main aim of Adventist Christian Education that leads students to discover and understand the truth through critical thinking.
